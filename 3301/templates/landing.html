What is C3301?

C3301 is a new class of digital asset where value is derived from intellectual effort. Unlike Bitcoin, where new coins are mined by computers, new C3301 tokens can only be discovered by people solving increasingly difficult cryptographic puzzles. With a fixed supply of only 3,301 tokens, each coin is a permanent, on-chain trophy of ingenuity.
